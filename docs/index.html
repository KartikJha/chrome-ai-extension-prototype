Chrome AI summarizer extension overview

Explore the features and use-cases of AI summarizer.

About the Extension

This Chrome Extension simplifies your browsing experience by providing a lightweight summarizer built on top of native google gemini nano LLM that is available on device. Simple UI makes sure you are getting all the gist of current webpage without reading it word to word!

Key Features

Unlimited Summarization

Research effortlessly and stay of top of your goals with unlimited summarizations, no rate limits ;]

Privacy

Protect your browsing data with on device AI and secure settings.

Use-Cases

Enhanced Productivity:Spend most of your time in getting work done and let the extension extract the crux of information needed for your research.

Privacy & Security:Keep your data protected with on device AI without sharing it with remote servers.

Technologies Used

  • Languages: JavaScript, HTML, CSS
  • APIs: Chrome Extension APIs, DOM APIs
  • Libraries: FontAwesome, Google Fonts
