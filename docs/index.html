Chrome AI Summarizer Extension Overview

Explore the features and use-cases of AI Summarizer.

About the Extension

This Chrome Extension simplifies your browsing experience by providing a lightweight summarizer built on top of Google's Gemini Nano LLM, available on-device. Its simple UI ensures you get the gist of the current webpage without reading it word by word.

Key Features

Unlimited Summarization

Research effortlessly and stay on top of your goals with unlimited summarizations and no rate limits!

Privacy

Protect your browsing data with on-device AI and secure settings.

Use-Cases

Enhanced Productivity: Spend more time getting work done and let the extension extract the crux of the information needed for your research.

Privacy & Security: Keep your data protected with on-device AI without sharing it with remote servers.

Privacy Policy

This Chrome Extension respects your privacy and is designed with security in mind. Hereâ€™s how your data is handled:

  • No Data Collection: The extension does not collect or transmit any browsing data to external servers. All summarization is done locally on your device using on-device AI.
  • Host Permissions: The extension requests host permissions to read website content in order to provide accurate and efficient summaries. This permission is strictly used for analyzing the text content of the page you are visiting and is never stored or shared.
  • Transparency: The extension does not include any tracking scripts or third-party analytics tools.

By using this extension, you can rest assured that your privacy is protected, and your browsing experience remains secure.
